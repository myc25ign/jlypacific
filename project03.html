Penetration Testing & Ethical Hacking

Vulnerability & Exposure Management

Continuous scanning, prioritization, and remediation guidance across your attack surface—from legacy systems to AI-connected workloads.

Threat Intelligence & Detection

SIEM, SOAR, and AI-assisted detection tuned to your environment. We turn alerts into actionable intelligence, not noise.

Security Governance & Compliance

Framework alignment (ISO 27001, NIST, local regulations) and practical policy design that teams can actually follow.

Zero Trust Architecture

AI-Era Security Integration

BCP & Incident Response Exercises

Tabletop drills, playbooks, and recovery simulations—so your team knows what to do when something goes wrong.

Red Team & Purple Team Operations

We work alongside your internal teams and technology partners to run coordinated red/purple team exercises, validate controls, and close gaps with evidence—not assumptions.

AI-Powered Offensive Research

Our research focuses on how AI changes the attack surface: prompt injection, model poisoning, automated recon, and adversarial ML. We test what others only talk about.

CSIRT Training & SOC Maturity

We train internal CSIRT teams and help SOC operations mature—from detection engineering and playbook design to tabletop drills that test whether your team can actually execute under pressure, not just on paper.

Security Audits & Framework Alignment

Independent security audits mapped to NIST CSF, ISO 27001, and sector-specific requirements—giving internal audit and the board evidence-based assurance, not a checklist exercise.

Digital Forensics & Incident Analysis

When something has already happened, we reconstruct it: forensic analysis of compromised systems, timeline reconstruction, and evidence handling suitable for internal review, insurance claims, or legal proceedings.

Our Approach

White hat hacking is our core—not an add-on. We combine offensive testing, EDR tuning, Zero Trust design, and BCP exercises into one continuous security program. Find weaknesses. Fix them. Prove it.

Building a White Hat Security Program

We help you build an in-house or hybrid white hat capability: hiring ethical hackers, defining scope, running safe tests, and turning findings into executive-ready reports and remediation plans.
